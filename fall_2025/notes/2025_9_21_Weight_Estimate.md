[This video](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/qJ2_iHSxl24) is the most optimistic I could find for the 720 brushless motors - It shows them giving about 29g of thrust, similar to ChatGPT's best estimate. Lower estimates are about 20g. Multiplied by 4, the best estimate gives us a total thrust of around 116g, which more than twice our estimated weight from above, about 50g. If we are able to supply around the voltage demonstrated in this video (4.1V), I think this motor would likely work, especially if we use the slightly larger props found on Amazon. The batteries I looked at supply 3.7-3.8V, so that is promising. 

Unfortunately, some of the videos below are not as optimistic about the performance (20g range), so it is a bit of a gamble. Either way, I think we would likely have a flying drone, but if we're in the lower range, an upgrade might be needed down the line.
