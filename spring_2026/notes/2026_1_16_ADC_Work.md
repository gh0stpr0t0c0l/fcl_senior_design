### 5. **Battery component mixes policy + hardware**

Your battery checker currently:

- Knows ADC unit IDs
    
- Knows channel numbers
    
- Knows attenuation
    
- Knows bitwidth
    

That’s a lot of **board-level knowledge** inside a feature component.

Cleaner split would be:

- Board/platform layer owns ADC setup
    
- Battery component consumes “battery voltage”
    

You’re not _wrong_ — just tightly coupling layers.

---

### 6. **Hard-coded channel instead of discovery**

```c
#define BATT_ADC_CHAN ADC_CHANNEL_7
```

Works.  
But you _already know_ ESP-IDF gives you:

```c
adc_oneshot_io_to_channel()
```

Hard-coding means:

- Board revisions require code edits
    
- GPIO changes ripple upward
    

Not a bug — just brittle.

That’s fine _now_, but it makes reuse painful.
